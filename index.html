Hello! My name is Bridger Herman. I am a Computer Science major at the University of Minnesota, focusing in visual computing.

I am interested in using computers to help people, and to help the environment. I belive computers are an extremely useful tool for helping people understand the world around them, and to aid in decision-making processes. Visual computing and visualization come together at the intersection of science and art, forming a powerful combination.

I am passionate about the outdoors, about my work, and about spreading knowledge.
